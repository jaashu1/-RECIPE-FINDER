## How to Use

1. **Browse Recipes**: Scroll through the recipe cards to see all available recipes
2. **Search**: Use the search bar to find recipes by name, cuisine, or ingredients
3. **Filter**: Click category buttons to filter recipes by meal type
4. **View Details**: Click on any recipe card to see full recipe details in a modal
5. **Save Favorites**: Click the heart icon on recipe cards to save favorites
6. **View Favorites**: Click "Show Favorites" to see only your saved recipes
